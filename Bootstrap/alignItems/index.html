Align-Items
(major axis is in the 'y' direction)
(minor axis is in the 'x' direction)

align-items-start (default) puts items at the top of the container in the 'y' direction


align-items-center puts items at the center of the container in the 'y' direction


align-items-end puts items at the end of the container in the 'y' direction


align-items-self allows us to change the location of individual columns in the 'y' direction
Note: we are starting with align-items-end
